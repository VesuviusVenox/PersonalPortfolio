About me

Greetings, and welcome to my portfolio website! My name is Yoan Nikolov and I am an aspiring web and UI designer. I am currently studying Creative Media and Game Technologies at the Saxion University of Applied Sciences. I have designed several websites throughout my studies using HTML and CSS, and a couple beforehand, using online building tools, with a strong emphasis on an intuitive and fluid UI. In my free time, I like to explore the world and travel to new places, read books or play and modify video games.

If You are curious to find out more, you can go through all of the things I have made, or contact me directly. Enjoy your stay!
