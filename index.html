Bio

About me

Greetings, and welcome to my portfolio website! My name is Yoan Nikolov and I am an aspiring 26 year old game, web and UI designer. I am a recent graduate from the Creative Media and Game Technologies at the Saxion University of Applied Sciences. I have designed several games and website designs throughout my studies, with a strong emphasis on an intuitive and fluid UI. The games were developed in group projects, where my role was designing core gameplay mechanics, documentation, play-testing, level design, UI design and promotion. All of them can be found under the websites and Saxion projects tabs. Apart from website building, I also have a talent in creative writing.

I have experience with working in Visual Studio Code, Eclipse IDE, Davinci Resolve, GIMP, Audacity and I know HTML, CSS, VBA and the basics of Java. In my free time, I like to explore the world and travel to new places, read books or play and modify video games.

If You are curious to find out more, you can go through all of the things I have made, or contact me directly. Enjoy your stay!
