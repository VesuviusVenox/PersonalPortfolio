Crowscape

A point-and-click puzzle game made as a group project for a university assignment. The game is programmed in Processing, a Java-based language.

My main role in the project was a an engineer, and along with the other engineer in the team, we programmed the menus and puzzles with a given, basic engine.

About half of the puzzles in the game are programmed by me. I was also a pseudo-designer and helped with designing some of the puzzles and establishing the main tone of the game.

None of the art, assets or music are produced by me.

Game can be downloaded here

Crypt of Ammon

Another group project game I collaborated on. This game is a physics-based puzzle game where the player must push boxes with a magical staff to certain pressure plates in order to progress.

For this project, I was primarily responsible for the website shown in websites, as well as the infographic, the controller schematic and a basic physics prototype designed in flowlab.

Art, assets, soundtrack and coding was done by the others in my team.
